EXPLORE PITTSBURGH

Duquesne Incline

This incline is a historical place that moved cargo by railroad. Today, it is a popular tourist spot for its transportation and history.

Fallingwaters

Designed by Frank Lloyd Wright, this house was built on the emphasis of "living with the waterfall." People can enjoy the architecture and nature.

Frick Park

This is known as Pittsburgh's largest municipal park. It is a great place for walks, hikes, and scenery watching.

Phipps Conservatory and Botanical Garden

In Schenely Park is this famous botanical garden that houses many exotic plants for the public.

Pittsburgh Zoo & PPG Aquarium

This is one of six major zoo and aquariums in the United States. It houses more than 4000 animals of 475 species and 20 endangered ones.
